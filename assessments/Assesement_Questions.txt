
1. Depression (PHQ-9 inspired)
Instructions: Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by the following problems?

Little interest or pleasure in doing things

Feeling down, depressed, or hopeless

Trouble falling or staying asleep, or sleeping too much

Feeling tired or having little energy

Poor appetite or overeating

Feeling bad about yourself—or that you are a failure

Trouble concentrating on things

Moving or speaking slowly—or being fidgety/restless

Thoughts of self-harm or that you’d be better off dead

Choices (Likert Scale):

0 – Not at all

1 – Several days

2 – More than half the days

3 – Nearly every day

✅ 2. Anxiety (GAD-7 inspired)
Instructions: How often have you experienced the following over the last 2 weeks?

Feeling nervous, anxious, or on edge

Not being able to stop or control worrying

Worrying too much about different things

Trouble relaxing

Being so restless that it’s hard to sit still

Becoming easily annoyed or irritable

Feeling afraid as if something awful might happen

Same 4-point scale as above

✅ 4. General Wellbeing (WHO-5)
Instructions: Over the past two weeks:

I have felt cheerful and in good spirits

I have felt calm and relaxed

I have felt active and vigorous

I woke up feeling fresh and rested

My daily life has been filled with things that interest me

Choices:

5 – All of the time

4 – Most of the time

3 – More than half the time

2 – Less than half the time

1 – Some of the time

0 – At no time
